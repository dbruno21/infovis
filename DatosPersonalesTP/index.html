Data description

Over the last ten years I had a total of 53 purchases directly through the Steam Store.

Dataset contains for every purchase its date, total price and the names of the games included.

On November 2017 store prices were converted from US$ to AR$. For this reason all purchases coming after have been converted based on US$ price at their respective date.

Games included in all 53 purchases were categorized by their genre. This is an estimation based on the fact that games often can be considered inside multiple genres.
